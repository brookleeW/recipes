easy peach cobbler

1 cup self rising flour
1 cup white sugar
1 cup milk
2 cans (16 ounces) sliced peaches in heavy syrup
1/2 cup of butter

1. Melt butter or margarine in 9 x 13 inch pan.
2. Mix together the flour, sugar, and milk. Pour mixture into the pan. Spread
peaches, including syrup, evenly around the pan.
3. Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 30 to 40 minutes, until the crust
turns golden brown. Let cool for about 10 minutes before serving
